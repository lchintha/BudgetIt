Privacy Policy for Budget It

Effective Date: 1st Jan 2025

At Budget It, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Data Collection and Use

Budget It does not collect any personal information from users. All data, including your recorded expenses, is stored locally on your device and never transferred to any external servers or third-party services. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, financial information, or usage information.

2. Data Storage

All information entered by users, such as expense details and categorization, is securely stored on the user's device. You have full control over this data and can delete or modify it at any time.

3. Bar Graphs and Expense Categories

Our app enables users to view their expenses in bar graphs and categorize them for better visualization and tracking. This feature is powered solely by data stored on your device, with no external collection or sharing of your information.

4. Security

As we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, the security of your information is entirely dependent on your device's security settings. We recommend you use device-level security measures, such as encryption or a password, to protect your data.

5. Third-Party Services

Budget It does not use any third-party services that collect or process personal data. We do not display ads, collect analytics, or share any data with external parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Budget It is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the app, and users are encouraged to review the Privacy Policy periodically.
